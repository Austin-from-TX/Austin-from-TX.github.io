UBrew

A social media site for connecting with other Home Brewing enthusiasts.

Built on a React-Redux frontend and a Flask-PostgreSQL backend, UBrew is the most recent project I have worked on. It features the creation, reading, updating, and deletion of Brew Recipes, comments on recipes, managing your current Brewing Rotation, and the ability to follow and be followed by likeminded Brewers. Searching for new recipes posted by fellow brewers is also a key feature.

Artizen

A Map My Run clone with an artistic twist!

Built with a React-Redux frontend and a Flask-PostgreSQL backend, Artizen leverages the power of the Google Maps APIs to allow users to create 'Art-Walks', a feature that dynamically calculates the shortest walking route between the user's selected street art locations. Implementing AWS Cloud services, users users can create, read, update, and delete photos and Maps coordinates for any new art they find, allowing fellow 'Artizens' to instantly enjoy them as well. Other features include commenting and saving Artwalks to your favorites library

NiceBytes

A clone of the popular GoodReads where users can search, rate and review top restaurants

Constructed with Express.js with Pug templates, NiceBytes shows off our group's ability to create a fullstack application without a dedicated frontend library or framework. Its features include the creation, reading, updating, and deletion of Restaurant Reviews along with a search and profile pages.

BandAid

An app for musicians who want to collaborate on crafting their songs

A solo full-stack project made with a React-Redux frontend and an Express.js backend. BandAid allows musicians to record their music directly in app, making it available for bandmates and collaborators to listen, compose and rehearse their own accompanying tracks in real-time. Users can use the search function to find their bandmates or fellow contributors
